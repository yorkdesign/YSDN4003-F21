Assignment 2

In this assignment, students will research and select a topic through the creation of a screen-based interface.

You should remember this topic will remain for assignment 2 to 5 in this class. Make sure that you are comfortable with and highly interested in this topic from the start.

15% 1 week
Evaluation Criteria

Requirements

Context

Students are encouraged to think beyond the notion of “problem solving” and to either overlap or blur the boundaries between multiple approaches to interactive design such as exploratory, utilitarian and speculative approaches.

The application developed will be an expression of the student’s personal exploration of interactive design and of the chosen topic, but it must have a precise goal/use, be engaging, provide users with a satisfying user experience, wonder, surprise and ultimately promote recurring usage.

Device and platform choice are flexible:

  • Mobile native
  • Desktop web application
  • Watch
  • Physical installation

Students will select a interactive topic of their interested for the next 9 weeks investigations

Approach

This assignment will focus mainly on the topic selection. To do so, students will:

  • Research topic from previous interest
  • Collect content and visual assets
  • Define a research statement

Outcome

The outcome should be exprime into a strong narrative piece with few Figma frames. Specifically, this outcome should answer:

  • What is the goal of your project?
  • Why did you select this topic?
  • What could potentially be your inspirations for this project?

Make sure that your research is well prepared, that your idea is clearly defined and that your descriptive sentence communicates your intent clearly supported with visuals.

Tips

The better this outcome is, the stronger our next In Class exercise will be.
