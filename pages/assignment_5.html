Assignment 5

For the final assignment students will focus on showcasing their digital experience.

The main goal should attract potential future customers, audience and future users.

15% 2 weeks
Evaluation Criteria

Medium selection

Various medium choices are available and possible for this assignment.

Example:

  • Website (interactive prototype experience)
  • Demo real video (or combined into a website)
  • Printed material (Posters, flyers, etc)

Tips

Be creative and original!

Content strategy

Content strategy will be a key core component of your showcasing experience. Make sure your text is convincing and strong.

Specifically focus to explain well:

  • Key core features with a clear explanation
  • What are the benefits/values of your projects
  • Contextual example
  • Speculative testimonials
  • Speculative "as seen on tv" or showcasing on Product Hunt with publisher logo review
  • Etc

Existing material

Make sure you leverage all existing materials done in this class for this project.

  • High Fidelity Mockup
  • UI Animations
