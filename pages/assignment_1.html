Assignment 1

For the first assignment, students are invited to adjust, improve or extend an existing digital experience.

This project will have students embrace a mindset that sees constraints as a positive element for unleashing creativity and adding values to an existing application.

20% 3 weeks
Evaluation Criteria

Requirements

Application criteria

Students will have the flexibility to pick one application choice across all available platforms: web, iOS, Android, tablet, etc.

The selection must respect the specific criteria of being an existing consumable digital product that anyone can access. It cannot be a fake, or early beta-access application.

Applications inspiration

There are various ways of selecting your application.

  • Students can pick a current mobile application they use on a daily basis. It should be one that they have noticed something frustrating about and that – as a designer – they wish to fix.
  • A friend or family member who frequently mentions an application they use and complain about because of current limitations with the app.
  • Investigate Apple App Store/Google Play/product hunt for digital products with common complaints.

As a designer, we always took for granted that all projects must embrace a mindset of applying a design from scratch.

However, for this assignment, students will focus more on redesigning versus designing.

Research statement

Once their application has been selected, students must define the problem and give an official work statement. This statement will help with focusing on what exactly matters.

  • The Airbnb mobile application allows travellers to stay at rooms owned by home and apartment owners. I wish the application allowed users to split room fees between friends right in the app. This would avoid having to personally collect money from my friend. [Statement] Abstract
  • Allowing multiple Airbnb iOS users to pay for a reservation. [Statement] Research

Tips

Ensure that the current flow issue is clearly explained, either with a screenshot, text or recorded material.

Design

The design phase is a critical part of this assignment. Specifically, students will now increase the value of the current application selected using their design techniques.

Process

A strong and thorough design process will be needed to complete this assignment successfully, which will include:

  • A low-fidelity with user flow adjustment and paper mocks
  • Mid-fidelity wireframes with digital black and white rescale mocks and an interactivity prototype
  • High-fidelity mocks with pixel-perfect colourful screens, an interactive prototype and animations

Attention

It’s students’ responsibility to ensure that the final material simulates the real experience by leveraging design language, such as font, colour, components and visual esthetic.

If the gap visually between the existing and suggested design is too large – the final solution won’t be as powerful as it should be.
